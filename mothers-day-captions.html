Questions people ask

What is a good caption for this?

“A grandmother holds the family's whole history in her hands and her stories.” is a strong place to start. The ones that work are specific rather than generic: they name the real feeling instead of reaching for something polished. Pick the line on this page that sounds like your actual situation, not just the prettiest one.

Are these mother's day captions free to use?

Yes. Every quote here is original and written on this site, free to read, copy and share. You can also turn any one into a picture card with the free card maker, with no payment and no card details, currently free. Because they are original and never misattributed, they are also safe to print on a card, mug or gift.

How long should a caption be?

For a quote caption, one line is usually enough. A short, punchy line reads before the scroll and pairs cleanly with a photo, which is why every line on this page is kept short on purpose.
