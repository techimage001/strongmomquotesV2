Questions people ask

What is a good gentle affirmations after lo?

“Your love did not have an expiry date, and it never will.” is a strong place to start. The ones that work are specific rather than generic: they name the real feeling instead of reaching for something polished. Pick the line on this page that sounds like your actual situation, not just the prettiest one.

Yes. Every quote here is original and written on this site, free to read, copy and share. Do affirmations actually help, or is it just positive thinking?

Used honestly, an affirmation is not magic and it is not toxic positivity. It interrupts the shame spiral that says you are failing and offers something truer to hold instead. If a line feels untrue today, say it anyway; that is how belief is slowly rebuilt.
