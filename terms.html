Your saved content

Favorites, your own quotes and your unlock status are stored in your browser on your device, not on our servers. Like every web app, the card maker needs a connection to load the first time and works offline after that. Because your saves live on your device, clearing browser data removes them; the app provides a backup file so you can protect and move your saves yourself.

Signup

The card maker offers three free downloads on a device, after which a free signup keeps it unlocked. You give an email address and click the confirmation link we send; the link is valid for 48 hours and a fresh one can be requested at any time. Once confirmed, entering the same email on any other device unlocks the card maker there too, with no password. Signing up is currently free, with no payment and no card details requested. Consent to occasional emails can be withdrawn anytime via the unsubscribe link or by contacting us.
