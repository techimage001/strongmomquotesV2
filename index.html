About this site

Quotes for strong moms, written for strong moms

Strong Mom Quotes exists because the words moms actually search for, quotes about strength on hard days, funny quotes about the chaos, encouraging quotes for moms who are doubting themselves, are usually served up as the same fifty recycled lines on every website, or locked inside subscription apps. We took a different route: we write our own.

Nearly every quote on this site is original. Our writers set out to put honest words to real motherhood: the strong mom quotes that honor quiet daily courage, the tired mom quotes for the nights when the tank is empty, and the short mom quotes that fit a caption or a card. When we do reach into the past, our classic mom quotes page draws only on classic literature, poetry and traditional proverbs, with the real source named for every line. You will find no invented celebrity quotes here, because a beautiful sentence deserves an honest attribution.

What can you do with the quotes?

Read them, first of all. Every category page is open, with no account, no app download and no limit. Copy any quote with one tap for a text message, an Instagram caption or a card you are writing by hand. And when a quote deserves more than plain text, our free quote card maker turns it into a picture: inspirational quote images for moms in Pinterest pin, Instagram square and story sizes, with backgrounds and fonts you choose. Whether you need single mom quotes about doing it all, working mom quotes for a colleague's desk, new mom quotes for a baby shower card, or mom quotes from a daughter for a Mother's Day message, the same three steps apply: find it, style it, share it.

Alongside the quotes you will also find daily affirmations for moms: short, first person sentences written to be said aloud, with dedicated pages of single mom affirmations, new mom affirmations and working mom affirmations. Put one on a card for your mirror, your desk or your lock screen and let it do its quiet work every morning.

The collections run the whole range of the job. There are strong mom quotes for the quiet daily courage nobody claps for, tired mom quotes for the nights the tank is empty, and encouraging quotes for moms for the days self doubt gets loud. There are funny mom quotes about the coffee and the chaos, short mom quotes that fit a caption whole, and a page each for boy moms and girl moms. And if you want words with some history behind them, our classic mom quotes page draws on literature and poetry, with the real source named for every line.

Honest by design

A few things we deliberately do differently. Your favorites, collections and personal quotes are saved on your own device, not on our servers; the only thing we ever store is the email address you choose to confirm, and the privacy policy says exactly that in plain words. We ask you to click one link in your inbox rather than solve a puzzle, because that single click keeps the list human without handing your visit to a third party. The card maker works offline once it has loaded, like every web app, so your saved quotes travel with you. And because your saves live on your device, Settings includes a one tap backup so you can move them to a new phone whenever you like. We also tell you plainly what this site is not: it is not a mental health service, and if motherhood is feeling heavier than words can fix, please reach out to your doctor or a support line in your country. Words help. People help more.

Everything here is currently free: the quotes, the card maker, the favorites, the daily quote. If that changes one day, nothing you have already saved on your device will be taken away from you.

Questions, answered plainly

Frequently asked questions

Is Strong Mom Quotes really free?

Yes. Every quote on this site is free to read, copy and share, and the card maker is currently free to use. There is no payment and no card details are ever requested. We keep the lights on with a few ads on the site.

Do I need an account to read the quotes?

No. Every quote page is open to everyone with no account and no sign in. After your first three free card downloads, the card maker asks you to confirm an email address. Click the link we send, and the card maker stays unlocked on that device.

Where do your quotes come from?

Almost all of our quotes are original and written for this site, so you will not find them recycled everywhere else. Our classic quotes page uses only lines from classic literature, poetry and traditional proverbs, each with its real source named. We never invent celebrity quotes.

Can I use the quote cards on Instagram and Pinterest?

Yes, that is exactly what they are for. The card maker exports Pinterest pin, Instagram square and story sizes, and you are welcome to post the cards anywhere you like.

What does signing up for the card maker cost?

Nothing. Signing up is 100% free right now, with no payment and no card details needed. You give an email address, click the confirmation link we send, and the card maker stays unlocked on that device. You may receive occasional mom quote tips and offers by email, and you can unsubscribe anytime.

Where are my favorites and my own quotes stored?

On your own device, in your browser storage. Nothing you save in the app is sent to our servers. The only thing we ever store is the email address you give at signup. Because everything lives on your device, use the one tap Backup button in Settings before switching phones or clearing your browser.

Does the card maker work offline?

Yes. Like every web app, it needs an internet connection to load the first time. After that it works offline, so you can browse your favorites and make cards without a connection.

Can I put my own words on a card?

Yes. The My Quotes section lets you write your own quote, save it on your device and turn it into a card in the same styles as every other quote on the site.

How does the daily quote work?

A fresh quote is ready for you every day in the app. Like every web app, it rests while fully closed, so your daily quote is waiting the moment you open it rather than arriving as a phone notification.

Why do I have to click a link in my email?

That one click is how we keep the list free of bots without ever showing you a puzzle or a checkbox. Nothing is stored as a real signup until you confirm, and nothing unlocks until then either. The link works for 48 hours, and if it expires you can request a fresh one in seconds.

I got a new phone. How do I unlock the card maker again?

Tap Sign in at the top of any page and enter the same email you confirmed before. The card maker unlocks straight away on the new device. There is no password to remember.

How do I unsubscribe or delete my email?

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can also write to info@strongmomquotes.com at any time and we will delete your address from our records.
